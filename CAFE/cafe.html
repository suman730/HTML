ABOUT THE CAFE

The Cool Cafe (abbreviated as TCC) is an Indian café chain owned by Coffee Day Global Limited, a subsidiary of Coffee Day Enterprises Limited. Coffee Day serves 1.8 billion cups of coffee, annually, in six countries.

In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we serve fresh made-to-order breakfast and lunch sandwiches, as well as a selection of sides and salads and other good stuff.

"Use products from nature for what it's worth - but never too early, nor too late." Fresh is the new fancy.

Opening hours: Everyday from 10am to 10pm.
